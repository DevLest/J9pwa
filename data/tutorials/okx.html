1. Go to okx.com

2. Click Register to make an account.

3. Sign up with your preferred way.

4. Click Buy Crypto > Choose P2P Trading.

5. Choose Your Preferred Crypto Currency on the Buy Tab > Filter the Ads by Entering the Amount, Currency, Payment Method.

6. You can shop which good offer you want.

7. Enter the Amount that you will Buy > Choose Bank Transfer as the Payment Method > Click Buy Now.

8. You will see the Order Details and the Seller Information.

9. Copy the Payee Information to make payment as instructed > Double check the payee information and complete the transaction within the time limit. The bank transaction is made out of the Binance between you and the seller.

10. Once the payment is made click on Mark as paid. The seller will release the crypto after confirming the payment is received, and the status will be updated to To be released.

11. You will find the currencies in your fiat wallet> Click Transfer Fiat Wallet to transfer the fund and you may now do the deposit to your Agame account using the withdrawal function from your crypto exchange acount to your agame deposit address.
